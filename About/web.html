About

A short story of me

Hello, i'm Hasna.I recently graduated from IPB University with a degree in Mathematics. My academic journey has equipped me with strong analytical and problem-solving skills, which I am now channeling into my pursuit of becoming a Quality Assurance Automation specialist. I am deeply interested in ensuring the quality and efficiency of software through automated testing processes. Currently, I am actively engaged in learning various tools and methodologies essential for this field, including scripting languages, testing frameworks, and automation technologies.

My mathematical background provides me with a solid foundation for understanding and analyzing complex systems, which is crucial in identifying potential issues and improving software reliability. I am passionate about leveraging technology to enhance software quality and am committed to continuous learning to stay updated with the latest advancements in the industry. By combining my analytical skills with my dedication to quality assurance, I aim to contribute effectively to the tech industry and help deliver robust, high-quality software solutions.

Thank you for visiting my website. I truly appreciate your time and interest. I look forward to the opportunity to share our experiences and knowledge with each other, and I hope this platform serves as a valuable resource for you. Let's connect and learn together!
